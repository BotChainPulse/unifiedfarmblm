1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners. Cookies help us improve your browsing experience and understand how you use our Site.

2. How We Use Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Site to function properly. They enable core features like page navigation and access to secure areas. The Site cannot function properly without these cookies.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our Site. These cookies collect information about visitor numbers, traffic sources, and pages visited. This helps us improve our website and services.

Advertising Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements. These cookies track your browsing habits to deliver ads that are more relevant to you and your interests. They also help limit the number of times you see an ad.

Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the Site to remember choices you make (such as your language preference) and provide enhanced, personalized features.

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. To manage your Google ad preferences, visit Google Ad Settings.
